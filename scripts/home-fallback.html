Arizona & Nevada Multi-Trade Construction Contractor

Canyon State Enterprises is a multi-trade construction company serving Arizona, Nevada, Utah, and New Mexico. From our corporate headquarters in Kingman, Arizona and offices in Phoenix, Bullhead City, Lake Havasu City, and Las Vegas, we self-perform twelve construction trades in-house, including roofing, stucco and EIFS, HVAC, plumbing, masonry, specialty metals, and full general contracting. With roots that trace back to A-2-Z Roofing Supply Co. in 1987, Canyon State brings more than 25 years of Southwest construction experience to commercial and residential projects of every size.

One Accountable Team, Not a Chain of Subcontractors

Most construction problems start where one subcontractor hands off to the next. Because Canyon State self-performs its trades, there is no finger-pointing between a roofer, a stucco crew, and a metals installer who have never worked together. You get one crew structure, one schedule, and one team that owns the result. Canyon State holds Arizona ROC License 353683, carries full general-liability and workers compensation coverage, and partners with leading manufacturers including GAF, CertainTeed, Carlisle, Firestone, Versico, Eagle Roofing, and Petersen Aluminum.
